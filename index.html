Malaysia's COVID-19 Journey

Embarking on 25th January 2020, Malaysia's encounter with the COVID-19 pandemic unfolded, leaving an indelible mark on our lives, and sadly, claiming millions of lives both here and abroad. Although the pandemic designation was lifted on 5th May 2023, the specter of the virus still looms, its mutational potential a persistent concern. Let's delve into its impact on Malaysia.
