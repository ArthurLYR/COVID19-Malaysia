The COVID-19 Pandemic in Malaysia

The COVID-19 pandemic in Malaysia began in Malaysia when it was first discovered on 25th January 2020. Over the years of the pandemic, the virus has had a significant impact on our lives and has claimed the lives of millions more both in Malaysia and worldwide. While the pandemic label was removed on 5th May 2023, the virus still remains dangerous as the potential for mutation is still high. With that, it is worth taking a look at its effect on Malaysia.
